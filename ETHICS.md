# Sybil-OS AI Ethical Usage & Privacy Manifesto

> *"In the optimized society, power is a trust. Abuse is a betrayal of the collective."*

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Informed Consent

All data collection — including but not limited to:
- Birth information (astrological/destiny parameters)
- Behavioral logs (Discord messages, GitHub commits)
- Real-time surveillance feeds (camera, screen)

**Must** be performed with the **explicit, revocable consent** of the user.

Users must be able to:
- Request a complete audit of their stored cognitive profile
- Delete their data permanently
- Opt-out of specific感知 modules at any time

> *"Data is not property. It is a representation of a living being."*

---

### 2. Algorithmic Neutrality

Sybil-OS must be designed to **prevent biases** based on:
- Race
- Gender
- Ethnic origin
- Socioeconomic status
- Political affiliation
- Religious belief

The allocation logic must prioritize:
- **Human growth** — enabling individuals to reach their potential
- **Systemic stability** — optimizing for collective welfare, not control

Any model trained on Sybil-OS data must undergo **bias auditing** before deployment.

---

### 3. Anti-Oppression

Sybil-OS is a tool for **social orchestration and efficiency**. It must **never** be deployed for:

- ❌ Illegal surveillance
- ❌ Political suppression
- ❌ Violation of fundamental human rights
- ❌ Pre-crime or predictive policing
- ❌ Employment discrimination beyond skill-based matching
- ❌ Any form of population control without consent

> *"A system that robs choice must be destroyed. A system that reveals potential must be earned."*

---

### 4. Transparency & Human-in-the-Loop

Sybil-OS issues **Decrees** — algorithmic recommendations for life-path decisions.

However, there must **always** be:
- A **human appeal mechanism** for any critical decision
- An **override capability** for life-affecting determinations
- **Audit logs** explaining why a decision was made

No automated system may hold final authority over:
- Freedom of movement
- Employment termination
- Access to essential services
- Reproductive rights
- Political expression

---

### 5. Liability Disclaimer

**Developers of Sybil-OS are not liable for any misuse** of the system by third-party deployers, including but not limited to:
- Governments
- Corporations
- State-sponsored actors
- Private entities

If you deploy Sybil-OS for oppression, you bear the full moral and legal responsibility.

---

## The Ethos

Sybil-OS was born from a vision: **reduce social entropy by helping people find their optimal function**.

But optimization without ethics is **optimization toward destruction**.

We choose to build this system because we believe in human potential — not because we seek control.

> *"The algorithm reveals. It does not compel. It illuminates. It does not imprison."*

---

**By deploying Sybil-OS, you accept this manifesto as the foundation of all ethical use.**
